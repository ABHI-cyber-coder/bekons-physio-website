Our Services

Comprehensive Therapy Solutions

From hands-on exercise therapy to advanced electrotherapy and holistic approaches, we offer a complete spectrum of rehabilitation services.

Exercise Therapy

Customized exercise programs to restore mobility, build strength, and improve functional movement.

  • Therapeutic stretching
  • Strength conditioning
  • Balance & coordination
  • Postural correction

Electro Therapy

Advanced electrical stimulation for pain relief, tissue healing, and neuromuscular re-education.

  • TENS pain management
  • Ultrasound therapy
  • Interferential current
  • Neuromuscular stimulation

Holistic Healing

Integrated wellness combining physical therapy with nutritional guidance and lifestyle modifications.

  • Nutritional guidance
  • Lifestyle modification
  • Stress management
  • Mind-body wellness
